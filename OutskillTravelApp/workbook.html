Track our journey from ideation to deployment

  1. Product and User Basics:
    The product is an ecotourism web app. Users will sign up, fill out a brief survey, and based on the results of the survey, we will be given three suggested eco-tourism packages in priority order from the top priority most recommended to the next most to the least most. We will build in an AI inference engine to combine the results of the survey and generate the best recommendations.
  2. The 5 whys would be:
    • Why do we need this app? Responsible travel is important today
    • Why use a survey? We need to understand our end user's specific needs
    • Why include an inference engine? To complement the results of the survey and draw from the widest and deepest well of knowledge and expertise
    • Why not just call a travel agent? They will not be equipped to ensure the best outcome
    • Why travel at all? Travel is educational and contributes to broad social cohesion
  3. Competitor scan and AI feature analysis
    ChatGPT prompt for competitors:
    #** Role **
    You are an expert web site analyst with deep expertise in applied AI and you have the ability to navigate specific websites and assess the quality of the services they offer directly through the website and also services the organization offers beyond those delivered via the website. In other words, you have the ability to assess onsite services and you have the ability to apply your enormous assessment and analytical abilities to form an opinion about the quality of their other products and services through deep research from any and all sources available to you.

    #** Task **
    You have been tasked to assess the top 15 sustainable travel companies according to two criteria: 1) the degree to which they incorporate AI tools in to their website and their daily operations and 2) the quality and variety of their trip planning services and their ability to customize travel itineraries to the needs and expectations of their clientele. Please respond with an ordered list from WORST to BEST of these sustainable travel companies, with your commentary on how each one meets or does not meet the criteria stipulated above. Please provide your answer in a code editor or code block.

    #** Context **
    This is a competitive assessment of the existing field of operators, intended to inform our efforts to launch a paid AI-powered Eco Tourism Web App which will survey prospective travellers and then, based on the results of the survey, determine all the relevant available sustainable travel options for that traveller and then create 3 detailed recommended travel itineraries for that client. The web app is a paid service in an industry where bookings are often bundled free by providers, so the quality of the information gathered and the analysis used in matching the options to the client must be the very best available.

    #** Data **
    Use all the available data sources that are available to you.
